That begs the question: Isn't there a more elegant solution?

And indeed there is one.


The Arithmetic Progression Series allows us to compute it neatly!

$$ S_n = \frac{n}{2} [2a + (n-1)d] $$
given aas the first term, d as the common difference, and n as the number of terms in the sum, we can find the sum of multiples of any number.
